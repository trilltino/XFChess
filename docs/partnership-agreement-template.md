## 1. Partnership Purpose

The Partner agrees to host chess tournaments on the XFChess platform, providing competitive chess opportunities to their members and the broader XFChess community.

## 2. Partnership Tier

**Tier Selected:** [Bronze/Silver/Gold]

**Tournament Fee:** [£2.50/£5.00/£10.00] per tournament

**Term:** 12 months from [Start Date] to [End Date]

## 3. Financial Terms

### 3.1 Revenue Sharing
- **Tournament Entry Fees:** 100% retained by Partner
- **Marketplace Sales:** [70%/60%/50%] to Partner, [30%/40%/50%] to Platform
- **Charity Tournaments:** Platform waives all fees, 80% to Partner, 20% to Platform

### 3.2 Payment Schedule
- Tournament fees collected monthly via direct deposit
- Marketplace royalties paid quarterly
- Detailed financial statements provided within 5 business days of month-end

### 3.3 Payment Processing
- Platform handles all payment processing and compliance
- Partner receives net amounts after payment processor fees (typically 2.9% + £0.30)

## 4. Partner Responsibilities

### 4.1 Tournament Organization
- Host minimum [4/8/12] tournaments per year
- Ensure tournaments comply with FIDE rules and regulations
- Provide qualified tournament directors and arbiters
- Maintain player conduct and sportsmanship standards

### 4.2 Marketing & Promotion
- Promote tournaments to club members and local chess community
- Display XFChess branding at physical events (if applicable)
- Provide tournament results and player feedback to Platform

### 4.3 Technical Requirements
- Ensure reliable internet connection for online tournaments
- Maintain updated computer hardware/software requirements
- Designate technical contact for platform issues

## 5. Platform Responsibilities

### 5.1 Technology & Support
- Provide tournament hosting platform with agreed features
- Offer technical support during tournament hours
- Maintain platform uptime of 99.5% or higher
- Provide regular platform updates and improvements

### 5.2 Marketing & Exposure
- List Partner tournaments in XFChess tournament calendar
- Feature Partner in platform marketing materials
- Provide access to XFChess player base (2000+ active users)
- Create co-branded promotional materials

### 5.3 Analytics & Reporting
- Provide detailed tournament analytics and insights
- Offer player demographic and engagement data
- Supply monthly performance reports
- Annual partnership review and optimization recommendations

## 6. Branding & Intellectual Property

### 6.1 Brand Usage Rights
- Partner may use XFChess logo in tournament promotional materials
- Platform may use Partner logo in platform marketing and listings
- Both parties maintain ownership of their respective trademarks

### 6.2 Content Creation
- Tournament games and content become property of Platform for archival purposes
- Partner retains rights to use tournament results and player achievements
- Both parties may create highlight reels and promotional content

## 7. Quality Standards

### 7.1 Tournament Quality
- Minimum 16 players per tournament
- Standard time controls: [15+10, 30+0, or 60+0]
- Proper tournament registration and pairings
- Accurate result reporting within 24 hours

### 7.2 Player Experience
- Responsive customer service for player inquiries
- Fair prize distribution and dispute resolution
- Safe and inclusive tournament environment
- Compliance with platform community guidelines

## 8. Charity Event Support (Gold Tier Only)

### 8.1 Charity Tournament Hosting
- Platform waives all tournament fees for charity events
- Provide enhanced marketing and promotion
- Offer transparent fund tracking via Solana blockchain
- Generate charitable donation receipts for tax purposes

### 8.2 Fund Management
- Charity funds held in escrow until tournament completion
- Direct transfer to registered charity organization
- Public verification of fund distribution on blockchain
- Annual impact report for charity initiatives

## 9. Term & Termination

### 9.1 Agreement Term
- Initial term: 12 months
- Automatic renewal for additional 12-month periods unless terminated
- 30-day written notice required for non-renewal

### 9.2 Termination Conditions
- Either party may terminate with 60-day written notice
- Immediate termination for material breach of agreement
- Termination for failure to meet minimum tournament requirements

### 9.3 Termination Process
- Final settlement of all financial obligations within 30 days
- Transfer of tournament data and player information
- Removal of branding and promotional materials
- Confidentiality obligations survive termination

## 10. Liability & Indemnification

### 10.1 Limitation of Liability
- Platform liability limited to tournament fees paid during preceding 3 months
- Partner liable for damages caused by willful misconduct or negligence
- Neither party liable for indirect or consequential damages

### 10.2 Indemnification
- Partner indemnifies Platform against claims arising from tournament operations
- Platform indemnifies Partner against claims arising from platform technical failures
- Both parties maintain appropriate insurance coverage

## 11. Dispute Resolution

### 11.1 Good Faith Negotiation
- Parties agree to negotiate in good faith to resolve disputes
- 30-day negotiation period before formal dispute resolution

### 11.2 Mediation
- If negotiation fails, disputes referred to independent mediator
- Mediation costs shared equally between parties
- 60-day mediation period

### 11.3 Arbitration
- Unresolved disputes referred to binding arbitration
- Arbitration conducted in London, UK
- Arbitrator decision final and binding

## 12. Confidentiality

### 12.1 Confidential Information
- Financial terms and revenue sharing details
- Player data and analytics
- Technical specifications and platform features
- Marketing strategies and business plans

### 12.2 Confidentiality Period
- 5 years from agreement termination
- Exceptions for publicly available information
- Required disclosure to legal or regulatory authorities

## 13. Compliance & Legal

### 13.1 Regulatory Compliance
- Both parties comply with applicable UK gambling regulations
- Adherence to GDPR and data protection requirements
- Compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
- Following FIDE and international chess federation rules

### 13.2 Representations & Warranties
- Partner warrants authority to enter into agreement
- Platform warrants technical capability to provide services
- Both parties warrant good standing with regulatory bodies
